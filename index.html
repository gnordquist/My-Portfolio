About Gabrielle

I have worked in the retail industry for over 5 years. It has taught me a lot, and I was able to work my way "up the food chain" so-to-speak, relatively quickly. Over 3 of the 5 or so years have been in a leadership role with a team under me. I loved this because I enjoy teaching and being able to rely on my team, and them on me. Working with others is truly gratifying, and has always been very rewarding- it is something I will always seek in a job of mine. However, I have come to realize that my current line of work isn't stable, nor challenging enough, so I thought, how could I keep it as my career?

I decided that I wanted to be the student once again, and learn something au courant, something completely new and relevant, several months ago. My newfound passion for programming developed quickly, and I finally seem to have found a forever challenge in an ever-changing field that excites me.
