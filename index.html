About Gabrielle

I was introduced to Software Engineering about a year ago through some of my family and friends. I knew of it, but never really knew the intricacies that lay behind it. Anything to do with a computer, other than the basics, was relatively foreign to me.

However, as the onset of COVID occurred, I came to realize that my (then) current career wasn't what I was looking for concerning my future. It wasn't stable, nor challenging enough, so I figured I would look into what Computer Science really was. I was intrigued. I watched many videos, read a couple books on JavaScript and HTML/CSS just to see if I liked it or not. After a short amount of time, I came out with a strong desire to learn more. I thoroughly enjoy coding - and now, I don't run away from computers anymore!

I decided that I wanted to be the student once again, and learn something au courant, something completely new and relevant. My newfound passion for programming developed quickly, and I finally seem to have found a forever challenge in an ever-changing field that excites me. As I go through my journey in acquiring my B.S, I hope to be able to be a part of a team as an intern to really get a sense of the responsibilities I'll have and the culture I hope to be a part of someday. I have excellent communication and interpersonal skills, along with years of experience working productively in teams. I also have real world experience in high stakes analytical problem solving from working in management in the retail industry for over 5 years. Through actions, I hope my work ethic and motivation will prove what a valuable member of the team I can be.
